How to make my best ever Brownie Recipe that comes together in one bowl in just a few minutes. They're rich, fudgy, and gooey with a perfectly cracked top and loaded with chocolate chips. These homemade brownies are super easy and truly irresistible you'll never reach for that box mix again!

You can opt to add nuts for texture or switch up the flavoring (see my variations below) but I love these brownies just the way they are, rich and chocolatey washed down with a glass of milk.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar (200g)
  • 1 cup brown sugar (200g), lightly packed
  • 1 cup flour (120g)
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder (75g)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (6g)
  • 3 eggs room temperature
  • 1.5 cup dark chocolate chips (260g)
  • 1 cup melted butter (225g), unsalted
  • 1 tbsp coffee (15mL)
  • tbsp vanilla extract (15mL)
